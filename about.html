Founder of WholeHealth

Dr. Amani Sissoko, born in 1985 in Bamako, Mali, blends traditional herbal knowledge and modern medicine: after graduating in Dakar, she launched the Health & Well‑Being SDG initiative in 2016, building a solar-powered dashboard aligned with UN SDG 3 her platform reached over 50,000 people by 2024, spanning nutritional education, mental health, and screenings via a network of 45 mobile clinics and 200 ambassadors, earning her the 2017 Africa Innovation Challenge, and embodying her motto “Health is a conversation, not a prescription” through open source, community led data managing local health outcomes.
